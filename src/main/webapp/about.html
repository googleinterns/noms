why noms?

for the environment

When catered campus events end, the leftover food needs to be consumed quickly or risk being thrown away. This kind of potential food waste is, environmentally speaking, the worst kind there is: food that has completed the entire cycle of growing, transportation, storage, and preparation—just to end up in the bin. noms helps divert some of the 150,000 tons of food destined for the landfill every day into the stomachs of your community.

for your wallet

Whether you’re a student or on-campus organization, you’re looking for ways to save money. As a student, we know you’re looking for ways to make tuition more manageable, and every bit counts. As an organization, we know that you would want the leftover food you spent money on to reach those who would benefit. noms provides the infrastructure to connect the goals of both.

for humanity

At some colleges, nearly half of all students reported experiencing food insecurity in the past month. noms offers your college’s community new ways to support those who need it most.
